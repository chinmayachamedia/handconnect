Why it exists

HandConnect started somewhere around 2am as a question: what if a webcam and a hand model were the only ingredients, and the only goal was to make something that felt good to be inside?

No account. No app to download. No data collected. Just a browser tab, your camera, and an hour of your hands making light. It's interactive art — quietly, without announcing it.

note — Built entirely with MediaPipe Hands, canvas, and Web Audio. The model runs in your browser. Nothing leaves your device.
